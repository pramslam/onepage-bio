About Me

I discovered editing video games when I played Warcraft II: Tides of Darkness. I made maps with the provided editor, but in 1998, my world was blown wide open with Starcraft and StarEdit.

My first real taste in game development was with StarEdit. I spent my youth setting triggers, tweaking gameplay, and testing hundreds of versions of my maps on the horde of Starcraft players.

Highlights

  • Newton Jump Experiment: Where jumping off a pad tells you how many newtons it takes to get off the ground.
  • Three Times the Charm: A fantasy home descorating game.
  • Holly Jolly Trolly: A twist on the classic trolly problem.
  • Galactic Bowling: A classic bowling game where you bowl the planet, on a rainbow, in space!
  • Lightspeed Lightsaver: An arcade game where you are a space ambulance and rescue astronauts.
